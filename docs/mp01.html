Task 5: Press Release 1 — Stranger Things (Task 5)

Stranger Things: a proven global hit ahead of Season 5

Stranger Things has delivered 2,967,980,000 hours of Top-10 viewing across 20 weeks worldwide. The series reached audiences in 93 countries. Among English-language TV series on Netflix, it ranks #1 by lifetime Top-10 hours. For context, recent leaders include Wednesday with 2,876,350,000 hours and Bridgerton with 2,279,710,000 hours. Season 5 will arrive with a strong and established global audience.

Task 6: Press Release 2: Netflix in India

Netflix in India: Local Hits Power a Breakout Year

Over the last 52 weeks, 55 different titles reached #1 in India. Locally resonant storytelling continues to lead the way: the longest India-only Top-10 run belonged to Sooryavanshi, holding for 18 consecutive weeks (2021-12-05 to 2022-04-03). Discovery breadth is growing too—calendar 2025 saw a -31.2% increase in the number of distinct titles appearing in India’s Top-10. The most recent India-only chart-topper was Mahavatar Narsimha (week of 2025-09-28). Together, these indicators highlight Netflix’s accelerating momentum in India and the expanding appeal of its locally produced hits.

Task 7: Press Release 3: Global Non-English TV

Global Non-English TV: The World Is Bingeing Beyond English

In the last 52 weeks, viewers spent 11,510,800,000 hours watching Non-English TV on Netflix—44.7% of all TV Top-10 hours (25,763,200,000 total). Audience choice remained broad, with 169 distinct Non-English series appearing in the global Top-10. Leading the wave were Squid Game (2,732,800,000 hours) and When Life Gives You Tangerines (481,600,000 hours). Year over year, Non-English TV Top-10 hours declined -3.2% (calendar 2025), underscoring the sustained global pull of stories told beyond English.
